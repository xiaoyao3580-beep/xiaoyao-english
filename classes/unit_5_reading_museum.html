Article 1 · UK Bans Multi-buy Offers

英国禁止“买一送一”等不健康食品促销。

The UK will ban “buy one get one free” promotions for food high in fat, sugar or salt and free refills of sugary soft drinks in restaurants beginning in April 2022.
标题抓法ban = 禁止；multi-buy offers = 多件购买促销。
核心表达buy one get one free = 买一送一；free refills = 免费续杯。
语法重点food high in fat 中 high in fat 后置修饰 food；beginning in April 2022 是非谓语补充时间。
阅读重点英文常先给主干结论，再用地点、时间、对象和数据补充。
Article 2 · Having a Cat Can Improve Your Quality of Life

猫不只是宠物，也可以带来陪伴、情绪支持和心理健康益处。

Having a cat can improve your quality of life because cats can form close bonds with their owners and give people someone to talk to.
标题抓法Having a cat 是动名词作主语，表示“养猫这件事”。
核心表达companionship / close bonds / mental health / reduce stress / combat loneliness。
语法重点someone to talk to 中 to talk to 后置修饰 someone，意思是“可以交谈的人”。
阅读重点先看作者结论，再看研究、数据和例子如何支撑结论。
